The Json2python extension is developed and maintained by roc (rockiachen@gmail.com).

Data Collection
The Extension stores settings locally in your browser.
It does not collect any personal data.
It does not collect browsing history, analytics, or tracking information.
No data is sent to the developer or any external server.
Google Drive Backup (Optional)
If you enable the optional Google Drive backup:

The Extension may save configuration files to your Google Drive.
It only accesses files created by the Extension.
It never reads or modifies any other files in your Drive.
Data Use
Data is used only to support:

Local settings
Optional backup and restore features
